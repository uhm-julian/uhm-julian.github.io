Competitors

Brands

Two big brands that you will be up against are Lindt and Hershey's. Both are very well known and delicious chocolate makers who will try to snatch away your business.

Strengths

Lindt excells at making high class chocolate for a fancy chocolate eating experience while Hershy makes the perfect evry day chocolate bar.

Weaknesses

Lindt alienates the lower and middle class by marketing their fancy chocolate. Hershey has very plain branding and not enough pizzaz.
