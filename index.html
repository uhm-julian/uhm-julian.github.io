Shuffle Images: Display Multiple Images on Hover is a wonderful plugin for creating a display that shuffles through a series of photos as you move your mouse over it.

The purpose of this plugin is to show many different photos within one display, one at a time and depending on the settings, it also allows verious triggers to activate the image switches.

You can control many different aspects on thsi plugin including how many pixesl you must move your mouse in order for the image to swtich, how long you have to hover on the image to activate the image shuffle and scroll trigger which sets a distance of how many pixels you have to scroll before the plugin activates.
